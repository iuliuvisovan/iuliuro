ABOUT
CuSEr is a web application and and API for the entertainment of a pub/coffee-shop's customers while they wait for their meals. CuSEr uses user feedback, reasoning, and machine learning to supply customers with comics, funny videos, or even games, to keep them entertained while they wait for their meals.
